Poetry Is

4/19/2021 • Rohan Mehta | Rhyming

    A goblet of glass,

        the honeysuckle-past,

    souvenirs of spring,

        a Friday’s fluttering


    whispers of a wishbone,

        a falcon of fog,

    droplets of daytime,

        and on, and on, and on


    the human heart,

        apostate of the mind’s machinations,

    a scavenger’s sight:

        an eye of the imagination
